DWELL — Threshold logic

Normal (teal accent): Yesterday's dwell ≤ 1.5× the day-type average
Flagged (amber accent + amber value): Yesterday's dwell > 1.5× the day-type average
Average calculation: Mean of all same day-type days in the trend window (up to 56 days = ~8 weeks)
